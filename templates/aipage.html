CIFAR10CNN is a Convolutional Neural Network trained on the CIFAR10 dataset. It currently has an 80% accuracy.
CIFAR10CNN is trained to recognize the difference between an airplane, car/automobile, bird, cat, deer, dog, frog, horse, ship and semitruck.
